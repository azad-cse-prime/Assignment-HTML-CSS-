Message from the Faculty Dean

Dr. Rahat Hossain Faisal

Dean, Faculty of Engineering

Welcome to the Faculty of Engineering at the University of Barishal. Our faculty is dedicated to fostering an environment of academic excellence, innovation, and research. We are proud to offer cutting-edge programs across various disciplines, preparing our students to be leaders and problem-solvers in the rapidly evolving technological landscape of Bangladesh and beyond. Our commitment lies in providing quality education, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive learning community. We look forward to your journey with us as we build a brighter future through engineering and technology.
